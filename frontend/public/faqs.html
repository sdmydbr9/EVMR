Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is PetSphere?

PetSphere is a comprehensive veterinary practice management system designed to streamline operations, improve patient care, and enhance client communication for veterinary clinics of all sizes.

How does PetSphere differ from other veterinary software?

PetSphere stands out with its intuitive user interface, comprehensive feature set, and modern cloud-based architecture. We focus on providing a seamless experience that integrates all aspects of veterinary practice management while offering superior technical support and regular updates based on user feedback.

Is PetSphere suitable for my clinic size?

Yes! PetSphere is designed to scale with your practice. We offer different plans to accommodate solo practitioners, small clinics, and large multi-location practices. Our flexible pricing structure ensures you only pay for what you need.

Getting Started

How do I sign up for PetSphere?

Signing up is easy! Simply click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, choose your plan, and follow the guided setup process. You'll be up and running in minutes.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card is required to start your trial.

How long does implementation typically take?

Most clinics can be fully operational on PetSphere within 1-2 weeks. This includes data migration, staff training, and customization. Our implementation team will work with you to ensure a smooth transition.

Features and Functionality

Can PetSphere integrate with my existing systems?

Yes, PetSphere offers integrations with a wide range of veterinary equipment, laboratory systems, payment processors, and other third-party applications. Visit our Integrations page for more details.

How does the appointment scheduling system work?

Our scheduling system allows you to manage appointments across multiple providers and locations. Features include customizable appointment types, recurring appointments, waitlists, and automated reminders to reduce no-shows.

Does PetSphere include inventory management?

Yes, our comprehensive inventory management system helps you track stock levels, set reorder points, manage vendors, and analyze inventory costs. It also integrates with your billing system for seamless charge capture.

Data Security and Compliance

Is my data secure with PetSphere?

Absolutely. We implement industry-leading security measures including encryption, secure data centers, regular security audits, and strict access controls. Learn more on our Security page.

How does PetSphere handle backups?

We perform automated backups multiple times daily and store them securely in geographically distributed locations. Your data is protected against hardware failures, natural disasters, and other potential issues.

Is PetSphere compliant with veterinary regulations?

Yes, PetSphere is designed to help your practice maintain compliance with relevant regulations. For more information, please visit our Compliance page.

Billing and Support

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers for annual plans. Billing occurs monthly or annually depending on your selected plan.

Can I change my subscription plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, with prorated credits applied for downgrades.

How can I get support if I have questions or issues?

Our support team is available via chat, email, and phone during business hours. We also offer an extensive knowledge base, video tutorials, and regular webinars. Visit our Support page for more information.
