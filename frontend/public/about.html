About PetSphere

Our Mission

At PetSphere, our mission is to transform veterinary practice management through innovative technology that empowers veterinary professionals to provide exceptional care while running efficient, profitable practices.

We believe that when veterinary professionals have the right tools, they can focus more on what matters most: the health and wellbeing of animals in their care.

Our Story

PetSphere was founded in 2018 by a team of veterinarians and software engineers who recognized the need for a modern, comprehensive practice management solution designed specifically for the unique challenges of veterinary medicine.

After experiencing firsthand the limitations of existing veterinary software, our founders set out to create a platform that would streamline workflows, improve client communication, and provide powerful insights to help practices thrive.

What began as a small startup has grown into a trusted solution used by thousands of veterinary professionals across the country. Throughout our growth, we've remained committed to our core values of innovation, reliability, and exceptional service.

Our Values

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in veterinary software, embracing new technologies and approaches to solve complex problems.

Reliability

We understand that veterinary practices depend on our platform every day, which is why we're committed to delivering a stable, secure, and dependable solution.

Collaboration

We work closely with veterinary professionals to ensure our platform meets their real-world needs, valuing their feedback and incorporating it into our development process.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from the quality of our code to the support we provide our customers.
