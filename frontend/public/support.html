Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link in the email and follow the instructions to create a new password.

How do I add a new user to my practice?

To add a new user:

  1. Log in as an administrator
  2. Go to Settings > User Management
  3. Click "Add New User"
  4. Fill in the user's information and select their role
  5. Click "Save" to create the user account

The new user will receive an email with instructions to set up their password.

Can I import data from my current practice management system?

Yes, PetSphere supports data migration from most popular veterinary practice management systems. We offer a comprehensive data migration service to help you transition smoothly. Our team will work with you to export data from your current system and import it into PetSphere, ensuring that your patient records, client information, and appointment history are preserved.

For more information about data migration, please contact our support team or visit our Data Migration Guide.

How do I set up appointment reminders?

To set up automatic appointment reminders:

  1. Go to Settings > Appointment Settings
  2. Click on "Reminder Settings"
  3. Configure your preferred reminder schedule (e.g., 24 hours before appointment)
  4. Select reminder methods (email, SMS, or both)
  5. Customize the reminder message template
  6. Click "Save Changes"

Reminders will be sent automatically based on your settings. You can also send manual reminders from the appointment details page.

Is my data secure with PetSphere?

Yes, data security is our top priority. PetSphere implements multiple layers of security to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • We maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance and adhere to HIPAA security standards
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing ensure our systems remain secure
  • Comprehensive backup systems protect against data loss
  • Role-based access controls ensure users can only access the data they need

For more information about our security measures, please visit our Security page.
