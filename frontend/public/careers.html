Join Our Team

Work With Purpose

At PetSphere, we're on a mission to transform veterinary care through innovative technology. We're looking for passionate individuals who share our commitment to improving the lives of veterinary professionals and the animals they care for.

Join our diverse team of veterinarians, engineers, designers, and customer success specialists as we build the future of veterinary practice management.

Why Work at PetSphere

Comprehensive Benefits

We offer competitive health, dental, and vision insurance, along with a 401(k) matching program to help you plan for the future.

Work-Life Balance

Enjoy flexible work arrangements, generous PTO, and a culture that respects your time outside of work.

Professional Growth

We invest in your development with learning stipends, conference attendance, and clear career paths.

Meaningful Impact

Your work directly helps veterinary professionals provide better care to animals and run more successful practices.

Pet-Friendly Office

Bring your furry friends to work! Our offices are designed to accommodate pets and the humans who love them.

Community Involvement

We support animal welfare organizations and encourage team members to volunteer with paid volunteer time off.

Our Culture

At PetSphere, we foster a collaborative, inclusive environment where every team member's voice is heard and valued. We believe in transparency, open communication, and celebrating our successes together.

Our team is united by a shared passion for animals and technology. We work hard, but we also make time for fun with regular team events, hackathons, and volunteer opportunities.

We're committed to building a diverse team that reflects the communities we serve. We believe that different perspectives lead to better solutions and a stronger company.
