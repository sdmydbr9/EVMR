Compliance

Last Updated: June 1, 2023

At PetSphere, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of compliance with relevant laws, regulations, and industry best practices. This page outlines our compliance framework and the measures we take to protect your data and ensure the security and reliability of our services.

1. Data Protection and Privacy

We take data protection and privacy seriously. Our platform is designed to comply with various data protection regulations, including:

GDPR CCPA HIPAA PIPEDA

Our approach to data protection includes:

  • Data Minimization: We collect only the data necessary for providing our services.
  • Purpose Limitation: We use personal data only for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Data Subject Rights: We respect and facilitate the exercise of data subject rights, including access, rectification, erasure, and portability.
  • Privacy by Design: Privacy considerations are integrated into our development processes from the outset.
  • Data Protection Impact Assessments: We conduct assessments for high-risk processing activities.

For more information, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

2. Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Our security framework includes:

  • Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. Sensitive data at rest is encrypted using AES-256.
  • Access Controls: We implement strict access controls based on the principle of least privilege.
  • Multi-Factor Authentication: We require MFA for administrative access to our systems.
  • Regular Security Assessments: We conduct regular vulnerability assessments and penetration testing.
  • Security Monitoring: We maintain 24/7 monitoring for security incidents.
  • Incident Response: We have a comprehensive incident response plan to address security breaches promptly.
  • Physical Security: Our data centers maintain strict physical security controls.

3. Veterinary Practice Compliance

Our platform is designed to help veterinary practices maintain compliance with relevant regulations and standards, including:

  • Medical Record Keeping: Our system helps practices maintain complete, accurate, and secure medical records in accordance with veterinary practice regulations.
  • Prescription Management: Our platform includes features to help manage prescriptions in compliance with relevant regulations.
  • Client Communication: We provide tools for secure and compliant client communication.
  • Billing and Financial Records: Our system helps maintain accurate financial records for compliance with tax and accounting regulations.

While we provide tools to help with compliance, it remains the responsibility of each veterinary practice to ensure they are meeting all applicable regulatory requirements.

4. Industry Standards and Certifications

PetSphere adheres to industry standards and best practices for software development, security, and data protection. Our compliance efforts include:

  • ISO 27001: We align our information security management system with ISO 27001 standards.
  • SOC 2: We maintain SOC 2 compliance for security, availability, and confidentiality.
  • OWASP: We follow OWASP guidelines for secure web application development.
  • NIST Cybersecurity Framework: Our security practices are informed by the NIST Cybersecurity Framework.

5. Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery

We maintain comprehensive business continuity and disaster recovery plans to ensure the availability of our services. Our approach includes:

  • Regular Backups: We perform regular backups of all data.
  • Redundant Infrastructure: Our systems are deployed across multiple availability zones.
  • Failover Mechanisms: We implement automated failover to ensure service continuity.
  • Regular Testing: We regularly test our disaster recovery procedures.
  • Service Level Agreements: We maintain SLAs for system availability and recovery time objectives.

6. Vendor Management

We carefully select and monitor our vendors and service providers to ensure they meet our compliance standards. Our vendor management process includes:

  • Due Diligence: We conduct thorough due diligence before engaging with vendors.
  • Contractual Safeguards: We include appropriate data protection and security provisions in our contracts.
  • Regular Assessments: We periodically assess our vendors' compliance with our requirements.
  • Data Processing Agreements: We enter into data processing agreements with vendors who process personal data on our behalf.

7. Compliance Updates and Monitoring

We continuously monitor changes in laws, regulations, and industry standards that may affect our services. Our compliance program includes:

  • Regulatory Monitoring: We stay informed about changes in relevant regulations.
  • Policy Updates: We regularly review and update our policies and procedures.
  • Compliance Training: Our team receives regular training on compliance requirements.
  • Internal Audits: We conduct internal audits to assess compliance with our policies and procedures.
  • External Assessments: We engage third parties to assess our compliance posture.
